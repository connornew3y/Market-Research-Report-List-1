<p><p>The temperature measurement robot market primarily includes infrared imaging and thermal imaging technologies. Infrared imaging utilizes infrared sensors to detect heat emitted by objects, providing real-time temperature readings without direct contact. Thermal imaging, on the other hand, creates detailed thermal maps by capturing infrared radiation from surfaces, allowing for comprehensive analysis of temperature variations. Both technologies are essential in various sectors, including healthcare, manufacturing, and security, enabling accurate and efficient monitoring of temperature for safety and operational effectiveness.</p></p>
