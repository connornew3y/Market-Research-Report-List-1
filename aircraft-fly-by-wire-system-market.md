<p><strong>The Aircraft Fly-by-wire System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Flight Control Backup Systems</li><li>Fully Fly-By-Wire Controls Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft fly-by-wire system market comprises two primary types: Mechanical Flight Control Backup Systems and Fully Fly-By-Wire Control Systems. Mechanical Flight Control Backup Systems utilize traditional mechanical controls as a safety backup to ensure pilot control during system failures, enhancing reliability. Conversely, Fully Fly-By-Wire Control Systems replace mechanical linkages with electronic controls, offering improved flight precision, reduced weight, and advanced functionalities. This shift enhances aircraft performance, safety, and efficiency, driving innovation in modern aviation technology.</p></p>
