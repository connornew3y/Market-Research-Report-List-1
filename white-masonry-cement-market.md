<p><ul><li>Type S</li><li>Type N</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The White Masonry Cement market consists primarily of Type S and Type N cements, each serving distinct purposes in construction. Type S is a high-strength mortar designed for load-bearing applications, offering superior adhesion and durability, making it suitable for various masonry structures. In contrast, Type N is a general-purpose mortar with moderate strength, ideal for above-grade interior and exterior applications like brick veneers. Both types cater to different structural needs, ensuring versatility in masonry projects.</p></p>
