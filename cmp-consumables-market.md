<p><strong>The CMP Consumables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMP Slurries</li><li>CMP Pads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMP consumables market encompasses two main types: CMP slurries and CMP pads. CMP slurries are abrasive mixtures that facilitate the chemical-mechanical polishing process, aiding in the removal of material from semiconductor wafers to achieve desired surface finishes. CMP pads, on the other hand, provide a substrate for this polishing action, helping to evenly distribute the slurry and enhance the effectiveness of material removal. Both components are crucial for achieving high precision in semiconductor fabrication and maintaining device performance.</p></p>
