<p><p>General Surgery Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) are critical surgical tools that use electrical currents to cut tissue and coagulate blood. The market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Monopole Electrosurgical Units** utilize a single electrode for cutting and coagulation, effective for a wide range of procedures. </p><p>2. **Bipolar Electrosurgical Units** feature two electrodes, allowing for precise tissue manipulation and minimizing collateral damage. </p><p>3. **Vessel Sealing Units** specialize in sealing blood vessels, combining cutting and coagulation functionalities for enhanced surgical efficiency and safety.</p></p>
