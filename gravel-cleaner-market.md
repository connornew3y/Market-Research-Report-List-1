<p><p>The gravel cleaner market features two primary types: siphon-style and electric gravel cleaners. Siphon-style gravel cleaners operate by utilizing a vacuum effect created by siphoning water, effectively removing debris and waste from the substrate while maintaining water levels. In contrast, electric gravel cleaners use a motorized system to agitate and suck up waste, offering more power and efficiency. Both types cater to aquarium enthusiasts, ensuring a clean environment for aquatic life while simplifying maintenance tasks.</p></p>
