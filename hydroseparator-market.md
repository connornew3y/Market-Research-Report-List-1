<p><ul><li>Pressure Type</li><li>Gravity Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydroseparator market is divided into two main types: pressure type and gravity type. Pressure type hydroseparators utilize pressurized systems to enhance separation efficiency, making them suitable for applications requiring rapid and effective removal of solids and contaminants from liquids. In contrast, gravity type hydroseparators rely on the natural settling of particles due to gravity, making them ideal for simpler and low-maintenance applications. Both types serve various industries, emphasizing effective liquid-solid separation tailored to specific operational needs.</p></p>
